Interactive Hiring Flowchart

Create Job Requisition
Create new job requisition in Rippling with required details. Exit: Saved requisition.
Set Pipeline
Confirm standard pipeline is attached. Exit: Pipeline set.
Attach Emails
Ensure each stage has the correct email template. Exit: Templates active.
Create Job Post
Publish job on JCC site + Indeed. Exit: Posting is open.
Application Stage
Review applicants. Send confirmation or rejection email. Exit: Reviewed.
Screen Stage
Conduct phone/resume screen outside Rippling. Exit: Screen complete.
Interview Stage
Manager schedules and conducts interviews. Exit: Interview complete.
Provisional Letter of Hire
Send Provisional LOH email and forms. Exit: Packet returned.
Background Check - Child Care
VDOE process; results to AED. Exit: Cleared.
Background Check - Non-Child Care
VA State Police + Fieldprint; clearance confirmed by Security. Exit: Cleared.
Offer Stage
HR sends full offer packet. Candidate signs in Rippling. Exit: Offer signed.
Pre-Onboarding Tasks
Complete IT/Security forms and send Welcome Email. Exit: Ready to onboard.
